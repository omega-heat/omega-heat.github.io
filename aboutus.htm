ABOUT US

Anand Engineering Service (AES) was first established in 1980 as one of leading company offering project solutions in the field of Edible oil Extraction Plants, refinery Plants, Hydrogenation Plants, Cattle Feed Plants etc. by Mr. M.L. Dayanad who was an Ex-Desmet pioneers in this field

Mr. Dayanand has completed at least 30 Oil Extraction Plants and Refinery in India as well as abroad and an active consultant to around 50 Refineries and Oil Extraction Plants in this country. He has long experience of over 40 years in the line of activity. Besides, he is a specialist in setting up new Oil extraction Plants & Refinery and has also undertaken project work in Indonesia, Iran, and Africa.

Mr. Dayanand, in association with Mr. C.N. Mohanan who is the financial head of the company acquired sound technical knowledge and trading experience for last 25 years in various machineries, spares, stainless steel and Mild Steel pipes etc. related to all sector of Engineering industry.

The above two persons are the promoters and directors of the above company, who have combined their technical, consultancy and marketing experience in the above fields. The third director of this company is a young entrepreneur, Mr. Devanand D. Malathu, a chemical engineer by qualification who has about 2 years experience in Ion exchange and has been associated with Mr. Dayanand in erection and commissioning jobs for various Extraction plants and refinery since 1998.

The other young and dynamic director is Mr. Anand D. Malathu who is a Mechanical Engineer by qualification and was the project in charge of Sherkate Kesht Va Senate Gol Bahar Tehran Iran completed in 2006 since then he is actively looking after the Production of the company.

We are only company in this field who enjoys the luxury of having the factory of 800 M² premises in Navi Mumbai. Moreover we also have an office space and godown in the Mumbai, being financial capital it is to stay close to the market and helps us to be competitive.

As on date we are the only company who supply spares and machineries not only to nearly 200 Solvent Extraction Plant and refinery but also to our competitors.

We posses a good Management structure to give our client an excellent service. The company already accure a well equipped Factory premises with trained skilled staff for design and manufacture of solvent extraction plant & refinery.
